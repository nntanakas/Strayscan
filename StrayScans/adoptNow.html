Adopt Now

The process is simple: The Center for Pets is ready to assist you in finding the best match with one of our adoptable pets! We welcome you to stop by anytime to visit and see the pets. Speak with an adoption counselor to learn more information on any of our pets, and spend time one on one with our pets to get to know them better. If you make the decision to adopt, we will guide you through the process that should only take about 20-25 minutes. Please make sure to bring a photo ID in order to complete the adoption process. Placing a pet on ‘hold’: We want your adoption experience to be as pleasant and stress-free as possible. We allow potential adopters to place adoption holds on pets in order for them to make arrangements to get to LHS, prepare their home for the new pet’s arrival, take their time making adoption decisions, and to introduce family members and other pets. Holds may be placed over the phone, via email or in person, and in the case of a “tie”, the individual physically visiting the shelter and the pet will be granted the first hold.
