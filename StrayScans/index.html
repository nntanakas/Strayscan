Strayscans

StrayScans is an application that focuses on the protection of city animals.​ ​ Users of StrayScans will be able to create an account for access to information provided by StrayScans.​ Take a picture of a stray animal and provide its last seen location.​ Submit an adoption form, in order to adopt a stray.​ , report lost pets along with last known location and a description in case they could not submit a photo.​ Aldo you can leave a comment on what the application might be lacking, or if they need help and send it to the administrators.​
